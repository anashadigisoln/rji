About Our Industries

"We, R.J. Auto Industries, were established in the year 1975. Our decades of experience have led us to become the favored manufacturer and supplier of quality Valve Cups & Collets. Our valve cups are designed to fit into the valve stems and protect against accumulation of dirt and dust. We offer a wide array of valve cups in different sizes. They are processed under strict quality control measures to ensure high durability and resistance to corrosion. Important features of these products are: Available in various dimensions, Highly durable in nature, Good resistance to corrosion, Tested for tensile strength, Offered in competitive pricing

Having served in this industry for more than Four decade, we have become the leading manufacturer and supplier of a wide range of Valve Cups & Collets. This has been due to the strong focus on quality and constant improvisation of our product features. The raw materials such as metal are sourced from well-known vendors in the market. We also apply various quality control measures at every production stage to prevent any defect and damage in the product. The parts are stored in a good warehouse and safeguarded from dust, moisture and heat.
